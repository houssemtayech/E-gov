    eParticipation
    The Information and communication technologies have advanced significantly and have revolutionized the social and political life. Given the many advantages offered by these technologies (speed, cost reduction, longer range), they are now crucial to the Public Administration, which notably uses to provide services and communicate directly with citizens. Thus ICT improves citizen participation by enabling citizens to interact better with the Administration.

    This is called eParticipation or electronic participation. The objective of eParticipation initiatives to improve citizen access to information, public services and enhance its participation in public decision making.
